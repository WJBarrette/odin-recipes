Burger Rice: A Barrette Original

Rating: 10/10

A clash of basic ingredients brings a tidal wave of Flavor.
This recipe is great because you likely have all the needed
ingredients in hand and it can be made in one pot.


Tilapia with Lemon Pepper

Rating: 8.5/10

This is an extremely flavorful and healthy recipe that can be made in
under 20 minutes with an air fryer. Throw in some fries or broccoli
halfway through the recipe and you have an entire meal.


Chicken Tikka Masala

Rating: 9/10

Although it looks like an intimidating dish, Chicken Tikka Masala is
actually quite easy to make as long as you have a spice rack. In this
dish, there's no such thing as too spicy.


Sinagang: The most flavorful soup

Rating: 10/10

This is perhaps the most unique dish on the list for an American pallette.
Sinigang brings a sour dimension to soup with tamarin powder, which you
can never overdo. This in tangent with its many vegetables and pork ribs
that melt in your mouth makes this option as healthy as it is delicious.
